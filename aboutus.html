ABOUT US

We are the Robo Vikings #9990, a dedicated and passionate FIRST robotics team founded in 2015 by our teacher and mentor, Ray Alford. Since our inception, we have remained committed to the values of the FIRST Foundation, fostering STEM education and inspiring the next generation of engineers, innovators, and leaders.

Throughout our journey, our alumni have gone on to pursue diverse and successful futures. Many have attended prestigious colleges such as NCSU and UNC Chapel Hill. Regardless of the path, the Robo Vikings alumni continue to make a significant impact in their respective fields.

Since the previous season, our team has seen a shift, with a majority of our members being from the younger classes. This has motivated us to put an even stronger emphasis on training the next generation of Robo Vikings while continuing to fulfill our team's responsibilities. Thanks to our dedication and teamwork, we have qualified for the State Championship five times. We are incredibly grateful for the contributions and commitment of all past and present team members, as their hard work and passion have played a pivotal role in our success.
